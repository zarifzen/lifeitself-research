Life Itself Research

We are a research collective exploring paths to a Second Renaissance and awakening societies.

We produce reports, papers, methods and tools. We welcome participation.

Research Approach and Outputs

Our work includes developing historical narratives and theories of change (intellectual, cultural, and systemic), sharing accessible ideas through the Newsletter and Wiki, and Mapping the ecosystem of regenerative initiatives. Some research is shared in the Forum, others as published White Papers.
Recordings of recent sessions and some written outputs can be found here.
We focus our research on four primary themes.

  • Paradigms and Worldviews

    We investigate the foundational assumptions shaping our societies; from metaphysics to modernity. This includes exploring spiritual, philosophical and cultural lenses that can support wiser ways of living and being.

  • Histories and Theories of Change

    We study past transformative moments to uncover patterns, leverage points, and myths that inform present possibilities. Our work bridges historical analysis and systems thinking to map how real societal shifts take root.

  • Ecosystem Mapping and Field Building

    We map emerging movements, actors, and ideas that are cultivating regenerative and post-crisis futures. These visual and conceptual tools help connect fragmented efforts and nurture coherence across fields of change.

How to join in

Come to one of the weekly hangouts which take place on Friday at 5pm European.
Details in the community calendar. If you're a good fit, you can then request to join the Research Whatsapp group, with plenty more opportunities for collaboration on research outputs.

FAQ

  • Can I come up with my own project or interest?

    Yes. We're very happy for people to bring their own projects and interests to the call.

  • How many people come?

    It depends but normally between five and fifteen with more at special presentations.
